Land Acknowledgement

Before we start I would like to thank and acknowledge the coast salish nation of xʷməθkʷəy̓əm (Musqueam), Sḵwx̱wú7mesh (Squamish), and səlilwətaɬ (Tsleil-Waututh) on whose territory we teach, learn and live. I would also like to say, that I recognize that this is unceded land that I live in and also know that the stolen land goes farther than where I live in.
